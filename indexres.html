About Us

"Ansh River Resort – Your home in the lap of Dandeli’s lush green paradise."

Discover the perfect blend of nature, premium, and adventure at Ansh Resort, one of the best resorts in Dandeli. Nestled on the serene banks of the Kali River, this adventure resort in Dandeli offers a rejuvenating experience for the mind, body, and soul.
