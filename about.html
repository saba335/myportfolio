About Ansh Resort

Ansh is a journey into the self, where you pause, reflect, and renew your connection with all that is accorded by nature and to the human spirit and body. Overlooking the pristine Kali River (Dandeli’s spiritual symbol), the resort is neighbored by Moulangi Eco Nature Park and Town on one side and the sunset paradise of Old Dandeli Bridge on the other.

Cradled by 2 acres of rolling hills and gardens, the resort is adorned with coconut palms, flowers, shrubs, and spices. Beyond the lawns is a vegetable garden and paddy fields, providing farm-fresh nourishment for guests. Inspired by the traditions and crafts of the Uttara Kannada region, Ansh Resort is your sanctuary for spiritual enlightenment and self-discovery.
