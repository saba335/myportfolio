What do data analysts do?

Data analysts gather, interpret, and communicate data to help organizations make better business decisions. Here are some of the tasks they perform:

Data collection
Data analysts collect data using surveys, software, and other tools. They may also buy data sets from specialists.
Data organization
Data analysts reorganize data into a format that can be easily read by humans or machines.
Data analysis
Data analysts use statistical tools to analyze data and identify trends, patterns, and anomalies.
Communication
Data analysts communicate their findings to others using reports, dashboards, presentations, and meetings. They need to be able to share their findings clearly with non-technical people.
Problem solving
Data analysts use their findings to help organizations solve problems and make better decisions.
Collaboration
Data analysts collaborate with others to identify opportunities for improvement, recommend system modifications, and develop data governance policies.
Documentation
Data analysts create documentation that helps others understand the data analysis process.


Data analysts need a variety of skills, including:

Mathematical ability
Problem-solving skills
Logical and methodical approach
Accuracy and attention to detail
Interpersonal skills
Teamworking skills
Written and verbal communication skills

Data analysts are in high demand across many industries.

What does a digital marketing agency do?

A digital marketing agency helps businesses improve their online presence and increase revenue by providing a range of digital marketing services:

Strategy
A digital marketing agency can help create a strategy from scratch or execute on an existing one.
Content
They can create and publish content for blogs and other publications.
Search engine optimization (SEO)
They can improve a website's ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs).
Email marketing
They can create email marketing campaigns to engage customers, generate leads, and boost sales.
Paid advertising
They can create ads and bid to have them shown when specific search terms are entered.
Social media
They can manage social media accounts.
Data analysis
They can analyze customer behavior and engagement patterns to make predictions about what might work in the future.
Market research
They can perform market research and analyze competitors' digital landscape.

Digital marketing agencies can work with clients on a one-time or ongoing basis. They typically employ experts in specific areas of digital marketing.
